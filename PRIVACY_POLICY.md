# Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Final ROM ("the app") handles your information.

## Summary

Final ROM does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All
processing happens locally on your device.

## Data we collect

None. The app does not require an account, and it does not ask for any personal
information.

## Internet access

Final ROM works fully offline. It does not send your files, file names, or any
usage information to us or to any third party.

## Files on your device

The app reads the files you choose so it can process them, and it writes the
output files you request. These files stay on your device. The app does not
upload them anywhere. You remain in control of your files at all times.

## Permissions

The app may request access to your device storage so it can open the files you
select and save the results. This access is used only for the operation you
ask for.

## Third-party services

Final ROM does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking
services.

## Children's privacy

The app does not collect any data from anyone, including children.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be provided with the app and
the date at the top will be updated.
